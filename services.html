Our Services

From optical design through prototyping to volume production — SPIO Systems supports you at every stage of the product lifecycle.

Turn-Key Supplier

End-to-End Optical Manufacturing

SPIO Systems offers low cost, scalable contract manufacturing of optical devices based on our Stacked Planar Integrated Optics technology. We act as your complete turn-key partner — from the first design sketch to mass-production-ready modules.

Whether you need to redesign an existing optical device, build a prototype to validate a concept, or scale to high-volume production, our team has the expertise and equipment to deliver.

Three Core Services

Supporting You at Every Stage

Our services are structured around your journey from concept to production.

Step 01

Design Services

Support the Customer

Our design team works closely with you to understand your optical requirements and translate them into a manufacturable SPIO module. Whether you are starting from scratch or looking to improve an existing product, we have the Zemax simulation expertise and optical engineering knowledge to get it right.

What’s Included

› Redesign existing optical devices
› Design new applications from requirements
› Source mastering solutions
› Zemax simulations & 3D modelling
🔬
Step 02

Prototyping

Get to Market Quickly

We rapidly turn designs into physical prototypes so you can validate optical performance and iterate fast. Our prototyping service bridges the gap between design and mass production, ensuring that your product is ready before you commit to volume.

What’s Included

› Rapid assessment of feasibility
› Masters and replicas
› Small-batch test series
› Preparing for volume production
🏭
Step 03

Volume Production

Build to Scale

Once your design is validated, we scale to high-volume contract manufacturing using our automated nanoimprint fabrication line. We handle type approvals, reliability testing, quality control, and packaging — delivering fully finished modules ready for your end product.

What’s Included

› Contract manufacturing services
› Automated volume fabrication
› Type approvals & reliability tests
› Quality control & packaging
