VIS Spectrometer

Visible-range spectral sensing on a single micro optics chip — a polymer photonics micro-spectrometer for colour measurement, spectral analysis, and embedded optical sensing.

Compact Visible Spectroscopy

Add the product overview here — what the VIS spectrometer is, the problem it solves, and what makes the SPIO chip-based approach different.

The SPIO VIS spectrometer delivers visible-light spectral analysis in an optical-chip footprint, built on our Stacked Planar Integrated Optics platform. It enables accurate colour and spectral measurement without a traditional optical bench.

Typical applications include colour and appearance measurement, point-of-care diagnostics, and embedded spectral sensing in consumer and laboratory devices.
