Our Technology

The World’s Smallest Micro Optics

SPIO — Stacked Planar Integrated Optics — is a polymer photonics production technology that enables volume production of micro optics, micro-spectrometers, and other small-size optical devices with photonic component integration.

Using nanoimprint lithography and a roll-to-plate approach, we print micro optics in polymer on glass wafers, then align and stack them to micrometer precision. The result: fully passive optical chips measuring only a few millimetres.

Learn About Our Technology →

📃 Optical Design

Zemax simulations, 3D models, performance optimisation, tolerancing, and size reduction — converting your free-space optical setup into a compact SPIO module.

🧰 Nanoimprint Production

Roll-to-plate imprint technology for high-throughput manufacturing. Lenses, mirrors, gratings, optical coatings, and freeform shapes on wafer.

🔍 Testing & Validation

Laser beam tracing, polarisation analysis, coherence testing, collimation verification, and lifetime studies ensure module quality.
