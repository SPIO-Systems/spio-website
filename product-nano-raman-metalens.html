Nano-Raman Metalens

A funded research project developing a nanostructured metalens for Raman spectroscopy — flat optics for the next generation of spectral instruments.

Funded Project The Project

Flat Optics for Raman

Add the project overview here — the research goal, the funding programme and partners, and the scientific approach behind the nano-Raman metalens.

This research project explores nanostructured metalenses — flat, sub-wavelength optical surfaces — as a route to compact, high-performance Raman spectroscopy. By replacing conventional curved optics with a metasurface, we aim to shrink the optical path while preserving signal quality.

The work builds on SPIO’s expertise in nanoimprint fabrication and wafer-level optics, extending it toward the nanoscale structures that flat optics require.
