About SPIO Systems

An optics company based north of Copenhagen — on a mission to move optics out of the box and into the future.

Who We Are

Redefining How Optical Devices Are Built

SPIO Systems is an optics company based north of Copenhagen, Denmark, founded in 2020. As a pure OEM business, we specialise in scalable optical chip technology — redefining how optical devices are designed, manufactured, and applied.

Our mission is to move optics out of the box with miniature, frameless, and mass-producible optical modules that can be integrated into everything from agricultural tools and medical instruments to wearables and mobile devices.

We are a team of engineers and physicists driven by curiosity, precision, and a vision to broaden the scope of optical sensing. By replacing bigger, manually assembled systems with miniaturised chip-based solutions, we open the door to new applications, new markets, and new possibilities.

Mission & Vision

The Optical Chip Revolution

Just as the electronic chip transformed the world starting in the 1960s, SPIO Systems is here to transform how optical devices are designed and manufactured.

💡

Our Mission

To contribute to the optical chip revolution — moving optics from bulky, manually assembled frames into compact, integrated chips that are miniature, robust, and mass-producible.

🎯

Our Vision

To become the technology leader in advanced optical systems based on Stacked Planar Integrated Optics and nanoscale 3D structures — broadening the scope of optical sensing worldwide.

⚙

Our Values

Curiosity, precision, and a long-term technological vision. We push boundaries and embrace the complexity of optics — because that’s where the most valuable opportunities live.

Our History

Rooted in Danish Optical Industry Expertise

Founded in 2020, SPIO Systems draws from decades of Danish optical industry expertise. Our founders previously worked at Kaleido, Millpond, and Ibsen Photonics, bringing deep knowledge in optical design, precision manufacturing, and component alignment.

This heritage gives us a unique foundation — first-hand experience of what it takes to manufacture reliable optical products, combined with a fresh approach to making that process faster, smaller, and more accessible.

We have partnered with Stensborg, a leading roll-to-plate replication machine manufacturer, to power our nanoimprint production line — a collaboration that sits at the heart of our manufacturing capability.

2020

Company Founded
